THE ENIAC CLUB

The official computer club at Bluebells School International has been initiated and managed completely by us, the students. ENIAC officially means Electronic Numerical Integrator and Calculator, but to us it is much more than that. It means Encoding, Networking, Innovating, Achieving and Computing. Overall it is a high octane filled, adrenaline rush, powered by the brains of the members of the club. Our previous events, VIRUS 2001, 2003 and Enter-o-Virus 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009 and 2015 were a roaring success, thanks to all the participating teams.

Events


PROGRAMMING

Number of participants: 2
Software: Turbo C++ 4.5
Time Allotted: 120 Minutes
Eligibility: Open

A 30 minutes objective type preliminary round to find the finalists among you. The prelims would consist of an MCQ, one word answer questions and an output finding round, participants are requested to bring HB pencil set with eraser. Prelims winners will sit for the final round that will be hosted on codechef.com

VIRTUAL TREASURE HUNT

Number of participants: 2
Eligibility: XI and XII
Softwares: Expect anything

A theme based event where participants will take part in a high speed chase of clues and hints on a computer using multiple software to reach the final answer before everyone else.The participants are requested to carry a set of headphones/earphones as these won't be provided during the competition.⁠⁠

PHOTOGRAPHY

Number of participants: 1
Eligibility: Open
Time: 2 hours

A theme will be given on the spot and the students will have 120 minutes to click and submit their best relevant and unedited picture, taken inside the school campus.

GAMING

Number of participants: 2
Eligibility: Open
Type: Console Gaming


Rules will be intimated to the participants on the spot. Participants must be prepared to face any kind of game.

POWERPOINT PRESENTATION

Number of participants: 2
Eligibility: Class IV and V
Time: 1 hour

The participants will be given a PowerPoint presentation, with each slide having some special effects. The participants have to replicate the effects in the least time possible

RUBIK's CUBE

Number of participants: 3
Eligibility: Open

Each participant can take part in a maximum of one event out of 2x2, 3x3 and Pyraminx. Rules will be intimated on the spot. Remember to carry your own cubes

QUIZ

Number of participants: 2
Eligibility: Open

The 30 minutes objective type prelims round will shortlist 6 teams for the final on‐stage round.
